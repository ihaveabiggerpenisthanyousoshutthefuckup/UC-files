Overview

OSPF is a popularly implemented routing protocol used in large enterprise networks. Troubleshooting problems related to the exchange of routing information is one of the most essential skills for a network professional that is involved in the implementation and maintenance of large, routed enterprise networks that use OSPF as the IGP.
