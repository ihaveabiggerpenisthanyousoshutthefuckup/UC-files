PPPoE Troubleshooting

After ensuring that the client router and DSL modem are connected with the proper cables, one or more of the following reasons is usually the cause of a PPPoE connection not functioning properly:

  • Failure in the PPP negotiation process

  • Failure in the PPP authentication process

  • Failure to adjust the TCP maximum segment size
