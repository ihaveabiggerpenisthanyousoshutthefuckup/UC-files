Step 5 – Verify that the VTP Clients Have Received the New VLAN Information

On S2, verify that the VLANs configured on S1 have been received and entered into the S2 VLAN database using the show vlan brief command, as shown in Figure 1.

As expected, the VLANs configured on the VTP server have propagated to S2. Verify the VTP status on S2, as shown in Figure 2.

Notice that the configuration revision number on S2 is the same as the number on the VTP server.

Because S2 is operating in VTP client mode, attempts to configure VLANs will not be allowed, as shown in Figure 3.

Use the Syntax Checker in Figure 4 to configure and verify VTP.
