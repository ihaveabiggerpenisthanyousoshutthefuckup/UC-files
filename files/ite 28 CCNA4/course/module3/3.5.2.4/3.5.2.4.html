The instructions are to drag each term to the field next to its appropriate description. The terms are as follows: eBGP, iBGP, BGP, AS number, single-homed and multi-homed. The descriptions are as follows: When the autonomous system (AS has a single connection to the Internet or another AS. The routing protocol used between routers in the same AS. Uniquely identifies the AS within the Internet. Protocol used for the exchange of routing information between autonomous systems, such as ISPs, companies, and content providers. When the AS has connections to multiple autonomous systems. The routing protocol used between routers in different autonomous systems.
